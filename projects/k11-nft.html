K11 NFT Digital Excitement

Role: Product Designer • Scope: UX, UI, Gamification • Year: 2023

Context

Designing accessible NFT experiences means balancing excitement with transparent ownership, costs, and custody.

Patterns

  • Onboarding with clear wallet steps and custody explanations.
  • Mint/buy flows with fee preview and confirmations; clear fallback and error states.
  • Engagement loops that avoid dark patterns; optional challenges with explicit odds.

Outcome

Higher participation with fewer support tickets, and improved understanding of ownership and transfers.
