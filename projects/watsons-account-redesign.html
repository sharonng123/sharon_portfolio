Watsons Account → Wallet Security

Role: Product Designer • Scope: UX, UI, States • Year: 2024

Context

Account management patterns translate to crypto wallet security: session management, device approvals, 2FA, and recovery.

Design Highlights

  • Security center hub: surface risk status, recent activity, and quick actions (lock, revoke, verify).
  • Device management: named devices, last seen, one-click revoke with confirmation.
  • Recovery flows: backup codes, hardware wallet pairing guidance, social recovery education.

Impact

Clearer mental models and visible controls increase perceived safety and reduce support burden.
