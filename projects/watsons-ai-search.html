Watsons AI Search → Crypto Discovery

Role: Product Designer • Scope: IA, UX, UI • Year: 2024

Context

Retail search patterns can inform safe discovery in crypto—helping users find tokens, NFTs, and protocols without confusion or scams.

Key Decisions

  • Entity cards with verification badges and risk labels (volatility, liquidity, contract verification).
  • Disambiguation flows for tickers and collections; default to safest match with explicit switchers.
  • Education moments inline: explain gas, chains, slippage and custody differences.

Outcomes

Improved discoverability with reduced mis-clicks and greater user confidence. Proposed patterns are adaptable to CEX and DEX contexts.
