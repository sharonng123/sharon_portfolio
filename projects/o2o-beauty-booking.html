O+O Booking → DApp Transactions

Role: Product Designer • Scope: Flows, States, UX Writing • Year: 2024

Context

Multi-step retail bookings share constraints with DApp transactions: timing, fees, confirmations, and reversals.

Key Patterns

  • Review step with fees, network, and timing clearly shown.
  • Explicit pending/confirmed/failed states with guidance and retry/undo options.
  • Receipts and history with export and dispute links.

Outcomes

Greater task completion and reduced confusion around fees and timing in transactional flows.
